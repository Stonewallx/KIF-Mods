Boss System - Changelog
# Version 1.0.0 (January 14, 2026) - UNRELEASED
---------------------------
[FEATURES]
- Different tiers of bosses to encounter. (Currently: Alphas/Elites)
- Elite Boss encounter system with rare/mythical/legendary species replacement.
- Alpha encounter system with stat boosts for double battles.
- Configurable random boss spawn rates via Mod Settings.
- Level scaling based on player party level for Elite Bosses.
- IV Changes and enhanced stats for all bosses.
- Epic backdrop and special battle UI for Elite Bosses.
- Item and money rewards for defeating Elite Bosses.
- DexNav compatibility preserves chosen species while applying boss modifiers.
- Boss System detects DexNav encounters and skips species replacement to preserve player choice.
- Elite Boss and Alpha modifiers still apply level boosts and perfect IVs to DexNav encounters.
